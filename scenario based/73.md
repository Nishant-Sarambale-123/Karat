What is Helm lint?

**Helm lint** is a command used to **validate and check a Helm chart for errors and best practice issues before deploying it to a Kubernetes cluster**. It analyzes the chart structure, verifies required files like `Chart.yaml` and `values.yaml`, checks for YAML syntax errors, and evaluates template correctness. This helps developers catch mistakes early, avoid deployment failures, and ensure the chart follows recommended standards. Overall, Helm lint improves the reliability and quality of deployments by ensuring the chart is properly structured and error-free before execution.
